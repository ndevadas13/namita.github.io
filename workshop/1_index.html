Melrose Township, Stearns County, Minnesota

Google

At the 2000 census[1], there were 772 people, 255 households and 207 families residing in the township. The population density was 19.5 per square mile (7.5/km²). There were 281 housing units at an average density of 7.1/sq mi (2.7/km²). The racial makeup of the township was 99.09% White, 0.13% Native American, 0.26% Asian, 0.39% from other races, and 0.13% from two or more races. Hispanic or Latino of any race were 0.39% of the population.
